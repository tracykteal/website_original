Call for 2013 LTER Training Working Groups
 
Data Management and Computational Skills Training for LTER Scientists
Proposal by Tracy Teal, Ethan White and Greg Wilson
A proposal with the goal of helping LTER scientists to be more productive and make more effective use of LTER datasets by teaching them basic computing skills and a general approach to working in data-driven science, by running Software Carpentry workshops at multiple LTER sites.
